# FutureRoots — Product Vision (Source of Truth)

**Tagline:** Building Generational Wealth & Memories

FutureRoots is a **Family Wealth Network** that helps families preserve memories, transfer wisdom, teach financial literacy, and build generational wealth through a child-centered family platform.

This document is the authoritative product vision. Do not redesign the product from scratch; evolve it from here.

## Core thesis

People do not need another crypto app. Families need a platform that helps them:

- Preserve memories
- Strengthen family relationships
- Build future wealth
- Capture family wisdom
- Encourage grandparent participation
- Improve children's financial literacy

Blockchain is invisible infrastructure. The product is not a crypto product — it is a family platform.

## North star

A grandparent should be able to:

1. Receive a child's milestone notification
2. Send congratulations
3. Contribute money
4. Leave a memory

**within 60 seconds, without ever realizing blockchain technology is involved.**

This is the guiding principle for every product and technical decision.

## Primary users

| Persona | Goals |
|---|---|
| **Parent** | Save for children, preserve memories, reward achievements, teach financial literacy |
| **Grandparent** | Stay connected, leave a legacy, contribute financially, record family wisdom |
| **Child** | Complete goals, earn rewards, build financial responsibility, create positive habits |
| **Extended family** | Participate in milestones, contribute to future funds, share memories |

Grandparents are a **primary** persona — the platform must make them feel valued, and their contribution flow must complete in under 60 seconds.

## Core product

Every child receives a digital **FutureRoots Vault** containing: family memories, photos, videos, voice recordings, educational achievements, contribution history, family messages, financial assets, and time capsules. The vault stays with the child for life.

## Product modules

### Family Graph (the core moat)
Entities: Parent, Child, Grandparent, Relative, Guardian.
Relationships: Parent→Child, Grandparent→Child, Relative→Child, Family→Members.
The more family members connected, the more valuable the platform becomes.

### Child Vault
Stores photos, videos, voice notes, achievements, messages, and future fund balances.

### Family Feed
A private family timeline (first steps, school awards, recitals, graduations). **No public social networking** — family-only.

### Achievement Economy
Parents create goals (read 10 books, practice piano, chores, exercise). Completion triggers rewards: cash, future fund contribution, badges, or family privileges.

### Grandparent Contributions (core growth engine)
Milestone notification → grandparent sends congratulations, contributes money, and/or records a video message — all in under 60 seconds.

### Future Fund
Child savings system. MVP: ledger, contributions, balances. Future: RESP, ETF portfolios, custodial investments.

### Legacy Time Capsules
Letters, audio, and video with release conditions: age 18, graduation, wedding, or custom milestones.

### Family Legacy Archive
Family history, recipes, stories, documents, photos — preserving heritage across generations.

## AI features (later phase)

AI augments memories; it does not replace relationships.

- **Family Story Generator** — photos/videos/messages/milestones → family stories
- **Legacy Book Generator** — family history books, children's milestone books
- **Wisdom Search** — search across grandparent messages, family stories, legacy archives
- **Birthday Memory Generator** — milestone summaries from accumulated memories

API-based models only (OpenAI, Anthropic, similar). Never host custom models.

## Blockchain strategy

Blockchain must remain **invisible**. Users never see wallets, seed phrases, gas fees, or crypto terminology.

Use blockchain only for: ownership records, legacy transfers, contribution proofs, asset portability.
Preferred network: **Base**.

## Business model

1. **Family subscription** — ~$9.99/month: family accounts, memory storage, child vaults, legacy features
2. **Contribution fees** — small fee on gifts, milestone contributions, future fund deposits
3. **Assets under management** (future) — ~0.5% annually
4. **Enterprise licensing** (future) — white-label for banks, wealth managers, credit unions

## Positioning

**Never** position as: crypto startup, blockchain company, wallet app, estate planning app.
**Always** position as: **Family Wealth Network** (alt: Family Legacy Platform).

Differentiators: competitors have either wealth OR memories — FutureRoots combines both, via the Family Graph, grandparent engagement, achievement economy, future funds, legacy archive, and the lifetime child journey.

**Unique insight:** most family products focus on memories; most fintech products focus on money. FutureRoots focuses on the transfer of memories, wisdom, relationships, and wealth between generations. That is the category FutureRoots is creating.

## MVP scope

**Must build:** authentication, Family Graph, Child Vault, Family Feed, photo upload, achievement engine, grandparent contributions, email notifications, time capsules, future fund ledger.

**Do not build initially:** trading, crypto wallets, DeFi, NFT features, complex investments, enterprise white-labeling, custom AI models.

## Compliance

The platform involves children. Required: **COPPA, GDPR, PIPEDA**.

- Children cannot create accounts independently
- Parental consent required
- Privacy by design

## Brand voice

Warm, optimistic, family-centered, trustworthy. **Never** use Web3, crypto, or technical blockchain jargon.
